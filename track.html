This Week

June 15–21, 2026 — The week featured two major international meets: the Doha Diamond League (June 19, Qatar) and the FBK Games Hengelo (June 21, Netherlands). Week ending Saturday, June 13, 2026

This Week

June 7–13, 2026. A blockbuster week headlined by back-to-back elite Diamond League meets — the BAUHAUS-Galan in Stockholm (June 7) and the Oslo Bislett Games (June 10) — plus the NCAA Division I Outdoor Championships at Hayward Field (June 10–13). The standout performance belongs to Yared Nuguse, who opened with a dominant 1500m win in Stockholm (3:30.11) and then ran a season-best 3:48.21 in the Oslo Dream Mile, briefly celebrating what he thought was his third straight Diamond League mile win before the photo finish ruled Timothy Cheruiyot the winner by thousandths of a second. Historic moment: the Oslo 5,000m saw three Americans break 12:50 in the same race for the first time ever.

Athletes

Yared Nuguse — USA — Mile / 1500m

This week: Won Stockholm DL 1500m in 3:30.11 (June 7); 2nd in Oslo Dream Mile in 3:48.21 SB (June 10). Two world-class performances in four days. At Oslo, Nuguse pumped his fist and flashed a "3" at the camera — celebrating what the scoreboard initially showed as his third straight DL mile win — before official results corrected the order in favor of Timothy Cheruiyot. Both 3:48.21. News: On a heater entering the outdoor season.
