Week ending June 6, 2026

This Week

May 31 – June 6, 2026. The week's headline performance: Gabby Thomas blazed to a 2026 world-leading 21.70 in the 200m at the inaugural USATF Lone Star Grand Prix in College Station, Texas — faster than her Olympic gold run in Paris. Trayvon Bromell also turned heads with a 9.85 to take the men's 100m at the same meet, edging world champion Oblique Seville. The Rome Diamond League (Golden Gala Pietro Mennea) on June 4 provided the week's other major action, with Noah Lyles running 9.88 to win the men's 100m.
