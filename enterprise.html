Enterprise Software Intel — Week of May 30, 2026

🏢 Platform News

S/4HANA

2026 is being called the "decision year" for S/4HANA migration — legacy SAP ECC use has now fallen below 50% of organizations for the first time, but 35% of larger enterprises remain in planning or exploration phases. Compatibility Packs expired May 31, 2026, removing a key ECC-within-S/4HANA bridge. Organizations not yet in active assessment risk facing premium consultant rates and reduced leverage as 2027 maintenance end looms. Full migrations for large enterprises typically require 18–36 months; the window is closing. Extended maintenance to 2030 is available at extra cost.

Joule

SAP Sapphire 2026 was dominated by the Autonomous Enterprise vision built on Joule. Joule Studio 2.0 is now available with free design-time access through end of 2026, enabling customers and partners to build, manage, and govern AI agents across the full lifecycle with embedded Vercel and n8n integrations. Joule Work was also announced — a dynamic workspace that delegates execution to AI agents. SAP's Autonomous Suite will deploy 50+ domain-specific Joule Assistants orchestrating 200+ specialized agents across finance, supply chain, procurement, HCM, and CX. Anthropic's Claude was named the primary reasoning model powering Joule.
