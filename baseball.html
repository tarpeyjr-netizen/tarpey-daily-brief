June 2026 Monthly Update — End of Season

This Month

The 2026 college baseball season has wrapped up for most tracked players. Emmett Christian (Tufts) is the standout of the group — posting an 8-1 record with a 2.70 ERA and earning All-NESCAC First Team honors on a 31-11 Jumbos squad. Logan Lowe (UNC Asheville) led all tracked hitters with 54 games played, 22 stolen bases, and a .402 OBP for a D1 Bulldogs squad. Season stats below reflect final 2026 numbers where available.
