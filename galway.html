Next Steps

  • Request video viewings immediately for any listing you like — Galway rentals move fast, especially within walking distance of UoG. Mention you are an incoming graduate student and include your start date (September 2026) and lease duration required.
  • Set up saved searches and email alerts on Daft.ie, Rent.ie, and Property.ie for each neighborhood. New listings typically post 4–8 weeks before the intended start date, so the peak wave for September leases will hit in late July and August.
  • Deposit process for international students: Irish landlords typically require 1–2 months' deposit plus first month's rent upfront. Transfers via Wise or Revolut work well; confirm the landlord's IBAN before sending. Never pay via Western Union or without a signed lease.
  • Prepare your reference pack: Letter of offer/acceptance from University of Galway, letter from a sponsor/guarantor (e.g., parent or advisor), proof of student funding or stipend, and a copy of your passport. Having this ready speeds up applications significantly.
  • University of Galway Accommodation Office: Contact the Accommodation Office directly for their off-campus listings board and student housing advice: universityofgalway.ie/accommodation
