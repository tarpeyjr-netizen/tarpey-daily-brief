Next Steps

  • Request a video viewing immediately for any listing of interest — good units in Galway rent within 24–48 hours. Email the agent with your intended start date, required lease length, and a brief academic background.
  • Set up instant email alerts on Daft.ie, Rent.ie, and Property.ie for each neighbourhood so you see new September 2026 listings as soon as they post (typically late July – early August).
  • Prepare a renter reference pack: university acceptance/enrollment letter, proof of funding (studentship, loan, or bank statement), passport/ID, and a personal statement. Irish landlords frequently request this upfront.
  • Budget for a deposit of 1–2 months’ rent payable at lease signing, plus the first month’s rent in advance. International students are often asked to provide a guarantor or pay extra months upfront.
  • Contact the University of Galway Accommodation Office for their off-campus housing database and student-specific support: universityofgalway.ie/accommodation
  • Check the University of Galway Student Union housing noticeboard and Facebook groups (search “Galway Accommodation 2026” or “NUIG Accommodation”) for private landlord listings that never appear on the main portals.
