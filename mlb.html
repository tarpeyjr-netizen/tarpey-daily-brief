Week ending June 9, 2026

This Week

June 3–9, 2026 — The week's biggest moment belonged to Braden Montgomery, who capped his MLB debut with a walk-off two-run homer in the 10th inning for the White Sox on June 9 — becoming just the fifth player in MLB history to do so. Off the field, Nick Castellanos was DFA'd by San Diego on June 3 (during a series in Philadelphia, his old home), ending a rough 39-game stint. Mickey Moniak extended his scorching run in Colorado, J.P. Crawford hit his 10th homer of the season, and several pitchers dealt with injury or roster setbacks.

Braden Montgomery — Chicago White Sox — OF

This week: 🆕 MLB DEBUT. Called up from Triple-A Charlotte on June 9 and immediately made history — singled in the 4th inning for his first MLB hit and RBI, then slugged a walk-off 2-run homer in the 10th to beat Atlanta 6-5. Went 2-for-5 with 3 RBI. Became the 5th player in MLB history to hit a walk-off HR in their debut. Pre-call-up MiLB stats: .313/.429/.606 at Double-A (27 G), .315/.417/.495 at Triple-A (29 G), 10 total HR. News: Montgomery was the centerpiece of the Garrett Crochet trade with Boston, a 2024 first-round pick (12th overall) out of Texas A&M.

Kaeden Kent — New York Yankees (High-A Hudson Valley) — 2B/SS
