Shark School
Music · Galway

Galway all-female garage trio Shark School — Nora Staunton (guitar/vocals), Peggy Ford (bass), and Meg Bruce (drums) — have quietly become one of Ireland's most talked-about live acts, and this week they announced their long-awaited debut album Selachimorpha, dropping 26 June via Dublin indie label Strange Brew.
