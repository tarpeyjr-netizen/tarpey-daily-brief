🔥 Trending in Ireland
Shark School
Music · Galway

Galway all-female garage trio Shark School — Nora Staunton (guitar/vocals), Peggy Ford (bass), and Meg Bruce (drums) — have quietly become one of Ireland's most talked-about live acts, and this week they announced their long-awaited debut album Selachimorpha, dropping 26 June via Dublin indie label Strange Brew. The announcement came alongside their blistering new single "Don't Trust A Man", a confrontational, jagged track that's been doing serious numbers online. Their sound sits somewhere between the deadpan chaos of Wet Leg and the raw crunch of Nirvana — twisted into something sharp, sarcastic, and distinctly West of Ireland. Nialler9 and the broader Irish music press have been championing them since their Galway pub circuit days, but the album announcement has pushed them into national conversation just in time for the summer festival season.
